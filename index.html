about

I was born in Seoul, South Korea.
Currently, I'm studying Painting and Drawing at OCAD University in Toronto, Canada.
I'm drawn to images of cyclicality, symmetry, and duality.
Fibonnaci spirals, fractals, and circles.
On the weekends, I work at my family’s restaurant.
